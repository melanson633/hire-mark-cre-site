# Revenue Map — Patterns → AI Monetization Plays

Ranked by `edge × market pull`. Pattern IDs trace to `capability_patterns.md`. Wiki signals trace to `wiki_ai-side-business_snapshot.md`.

## Tier 1 — Highest leverage, closest to current capability

### 1. Two-System Bridge (productized service)
- **Patterns:** P2, P5, P6
- **Offer:** $5–15K flat-fee build + $500–1,500/mo monitoring/maintenance.
- **What it is:** Pick any pair small operators reconcile manually (QBO×ServiceTitan, AppFolio×QBO, Toast×QBO, Salesforce×NetSuite, Yardi×anything). Canonical merged dataset + dashboard + scheduled refresh.
- **Why Mark:** Yardi+Argus build is the proof. Few freelancers deliver this with auditability.
- **Wiki tie-in:** "sell AI automations to local businesses"; `$200K in automation work` signal.

### 2. Clipboards-to-Code (vertical field-trade digitization)
- **Patterns:** P3, P7 (most underrated edge)
- **Offer:** $10–25K build + $500–2,000/mo retainer.
- **What it is:** Field-crew paper workflow → mobile app + ETL + dashboards. Verticals: small construction GCs, HVAC, landscapers, property mgmt maintenance, restaurants.
- **Why Mark:** Case 02 is exactly this — already proven with 20+ field-user adoption. Almost no AI freelancer has actually shipped this.
- **Wiki tie-in:** `$8k-15k jobs` landscaper signal; `AI prospector`; `monetize DESIGN.md verticals`.

### 3. Document Abstract Vertical (pick one high-$ niche)
- **Patterns:** P1
- **Offer:** $150–500/abstract or $2–10K bundles.
- **Candidates (pick one):**
  - Insurance broker submissions → underwriter brief
  - Property tax appeal packages (CRE-adjacent, recurring annual)
  - SBA / commercial loan packages for small credit unions
  - Vendor contract abstracts for SMB ops/legal
- **Why Mark:** Lease abstract pipeline = ~70% of the build for any of these.

## Tier 2 — Recurring revenue, lower per-client ceiling

### 4. Branded Report Engine
- **Patterns:** P1, P5, P7
- **Offer:** $150–500/firm/mo, sticky once embedded.
- **What it is:** License the data → HTML → PDF render pipeline to service firms. CPAs (tax estimate decks), wealth advisors (quarterly reviews), CRE brokers (BOVs/OMs), property managers (owner statements).

### 5. AI ROI Audit (top-of-funnel)
- **Patterns:** P5, P6
- **Offer:** $999–$2,500.
- **What it is:** Shadow a workflow; deliver before/after $ projections + 3 friction-removal recommendations. Converts to retainer or build (#1–#4).
- **Why Mark:** $800K and $103K outcomes are real Mark numbers — credible quantification.
- **Wiki tie-in:** `coreyganim` audit playbook; `$999 AI audit`.

## Tier 3 — Lower priority but defensible / equity-shaped

### 6. Skill licensing to platforms
- **Patterns:** P4
- **Path:** Expand Shortcut.ai relationship (equity, royalty, finder's fee). License vertical Skills (lease abstract, AM model refresh, draw recon) to Yardi ISVs, AppFolio integrators, JV reporting platforms.

### 7. Methodology product (cohort/playbook)
- **Patterns:** P5
- **Offer:** $1–3K/seat cohort or playbook on scoping AI automation engagements.
- **Why later:** Needs public case studies and offer track record first; the four cases are the proof seed.

## Explicitly deprioritized

- AI YouTube channel — opportunity cost too high vs. domain-expert leverage
- Generic prompt packs / cold-outbound automation services — saturated, no edge
- Generic AI prospector for landscapers — equal footing with non-domain freelancers; only do this *as part of #2*, not standalone

## Decision Frame

Pick **one Tier 1** play to anchor. Do not run more than two at once.

| If priority is… | Choose | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Highest $ per job + defensible niche | (2) Clipboards-to-Code | Hidden moat, almost no competition |
| Fastest sales cycles + recurring revenue | (1) Two-System Bridge | Closest to existing build, broad buyer pool |
| Shortest ramp from current skill | (3) Document Abstract Vertical | Lease pipeline already covers ~70% |
