# Public Asset Guidance

## Purpose

Store static files that should be web-addressable after build or deploy.

## Routing

- `downloads/` for downloadable templates and worksheets
- `newsletters/` for published newsletter HTML/PDF artifacts
- `research/` for published research packages and LinkedIn document assets
- `case-studies/` for public-safe case-study HTML/PDF artifacts
- `social/` for launch and social-sharing images

## Working Rules

- Use URL-safe lowercase filenames.
- Do not copy raw source evidence, private notes, or PII-bearing files here.
- Keep source-context notes in `docs/source-context/`, not `public/`.
